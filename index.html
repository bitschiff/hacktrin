About HackTrin

HackTrin V is the fifth iteration of Trinity School’s annual hackathon. Middle and high schoolers, of any experience level, can participate. You can form teams of 1-4 students, even across different schools, and work to make projects relating to coding. We'll have workshops and mentors to help you with your projects and learn! We will announce the surprise theme at our Wednesday night kickoff event the week of HackTrin, after which you can start coding, and continue until the end of the Sunday, March 4 event. You’ll present your projects to our panels of judges to win prizes in multiple categories (including a beginner category)!

FAQ


What is a hackathon?

A hackathon is an event where teams get together and build a project relating to coding, using software or hardware components, in a short period of time.

What types of projects are allowed at HackTrin V?

Anything you want, as long as it involves some coding component and relates to the theme. Popular projects in the past have included websites, apps, games, gagdets, or whatever else you come up with!

Are teacher chaperones required?

Yes, for insurance purposes, Trinity requires one teacher chaperone per school. Even if you have multiple teams from one school, you only need one chaperone. Please email us if that's an issue for you!

Is there a theme at HackTrin V?

We're announcing the theme for HackTrin V at the Wednesday kickoff event, as well as notifying all participants by email.

What's the Wednesday kickoff event?

On Wednesday, February 28, from 6:00pm-7:30pm, we're having a optional pre-hackathon event where we reveal the theme (we'll also email everyone the theme), give information about Sunday, and host some workshops! We'll provide dinner at the kickoff event. After the kickoff event, you can begin working on your projects!.

Who can come to HackTrin V?

HackTrin V is open to any current high school or middle school students. Middle school students will compete in a special division, HackTrin Junior.

What is different about HackTrin Junior?

HackTrin Junior is only open to current middle school students. HackTrin Junior also has separate presentations, awards, and prizes. The presentations will be held at 2:15pm instead of 5:00pm and awards will be announced at 3:00pm instead of 6:00pm.


Will there be food at HackTrin V?

Yes, we will provide breakfast and lunch, as well as snacks throughout the day. (We will accomodate any dietary restrictions.)
