About Us

HackTrin is run by Trinity School with the help from The Spence School. We encourage everyone from beginners to experts to participate in our hackathon. Teams should be 1-4, participants can create apps, websites, robots, and more. All projects will be presented to our panel of judges to compete for multiple different categories.

FAQ


What is a hackathon?

A hackathon is an event where teams get together and build a project relating to coding or engineering, in a short period of time.

What types of projects are allowed at HackTrin V?

Anything you want, as long as it involves some component of coding of engineering. Popular projects in the past have been websites, apps, games, gagdets, or whatever else you can think of!

Is there a theme at HackTrin V?

We're announcing the theme for HackTrin V at the Wednesday kickoff event, as well as notifying all participants by email.

What's the Wednesday kickoff event?

On Wednesday afternoon, we're having a optional pre-hackathon event where we reveal the theme, give information about the day of, and host some workshops After the kickoff event, coding can begin!.

Who can come to HackTrin V?

HackTrin V is open to any current high school or middle school students. Middle school students will compete in a special division, HackTrin Junior.

Will there be food at HackTrin V?

Yes, we will provide lunch, as well as snacks throughout the day. We will have options for any dietary restrictions also!
