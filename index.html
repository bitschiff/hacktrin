About Us

HackTrin is run by Trinity School with the help from The Spence School. We encourage everyone from beginners to experts to participate in our hackathon. Teams should be 1-4, participants can create apps, websites, robots, and more. All projects will be presented to our panel of judges to compete for multipal different catigories

This years theme is:

Anything You Want


You should interpret this theme however you feel is best!

Also feel free to use hardware such as arduions, body sensors, vr headsets...
